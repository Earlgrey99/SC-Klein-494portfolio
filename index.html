About Me

Hi, my name is Sofia Klein, but you can call me SC!

P1 - Metroid

I programmed the behavior for Reos, Zebs and Rippers, as well as designing and programming the new enemy type for our custom levels. I placed every enemy and upgrade in the NES level to be authentic to the original area. I implemented the in-game audio. I also made sure that the background music would change based on the type of room the player was in, and would also pause and play the item get jingle when the player picks up a key item.

Tools

Unity 2022.3.43f1, Visual Studio 2022 C# Scripting
